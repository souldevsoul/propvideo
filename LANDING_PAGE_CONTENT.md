## FAQ

**Q: How long does video generation take?**
A: 3-7 minutes depending on video length. 60-second tours average 5 minutes.

**Q: Can I edit the voiceover script?**
A: Yes! AI generates a script from your property details, and you can edit every word before generating.

**Q: What if I don't like the video?**
A: Regenerate unlimited times with different styles, voiceovers, or music at no extra cost.

**Q: Can I use my own music?**
A: Team and Brokerage plans allow custom music uploads. Agent plan includes 30+ royalty-free tracks.

**Q: How many photos do I need?**
A: Minimum 5 photos. We recommend 8-15 for a comprehensive tour (exterior, living areas, bedrooms, kitchen, bathrooms, yard/amenities).

**Q: Do videos include property details (price, beds, baths)?**
A: Yes! The voiceover includes all details you provide. Optionally overlay text on-screen with branding.

**Q: Can I download videos for offline use?**
A: Absolutely. Download in any format (MP4, MOV) and resolution (1080p, 720p).

**Q: Is there a watermark?**
A: No watermark on any plan. Add YOUR branding instead!
